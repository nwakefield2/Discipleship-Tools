# Discipleship Tools

**Clarity for the Local Church**

Welcome to **Discipleship Tools**, a specialized ecosystem designed to empower church leadership with **data-driven clarity and spiritual intentionality**. Our mission is to help churches move beyond simple rosters and into **meaningful, transformative relationships**.

This repository exists so that users of Discipleship Tools can **report bugs, request improvements, and help strengthen the platform**.

If you encounter an issue while using the platform, please open an **Issue** and provide as much detail as possible so we can investigate and improve the experience for everyone.

---

# Our Core Vision

We believe that the **local church flourishes when its leaders have a clear picture of their community's health**.

Discipleship Tools bridges the gap between **administrative data and pastoral care**, giving church leaders the visibility needed to ensure that **no one in their congregation is walking alone**.

---

# Key Pillars of the Platform

## 📊 Data-Driven Insights

Leverage your existing **Planning Center** data to visualize congregational health. From gender and age distributions to detailed attendance trends, Discipleship Tools transforms raw data into **actionable pastoral insight**.

## 🏗️ Organizational Clarity

Our **Org Chart** and **Branch Views** allow you to instantly visualize your leadership structure. Leaders can quickly understand **lines of authority, oversight, and ministry support** across the church.

## 📚 Kingdom Resource Library

Collaborate on **Bible studies, icebreakers, and teaching methodologies**. Our AI assistant helps refine theological analysis and can adapt adult lessons for **children’s ministry or other teaching contexts**.

## 🤝 Discipleship Network

Track intentional **mentorship and discipleship relationships** across your church. Built around the **“4-Person Rule,”** our network tools help ensure mentors maintain the capacity to invest deeply in the people they are discipling.

---

# Privacy & Data Integrity

Protecting member privacy is a core design principle.

Sensitive contact information such as **emails and phone numbers is never stored in our database**. When needed, this information is **securely retrieved in real time from Planning Center**, ensuring both accessibility and privacy.

---

# Reporting Bugs

If you encounter a bug or unexpected behavior, please open an **Issue** and include:

- A clear description of the problem  
- Steps to reproduce the issue  
- What you expected to happen  
- What actually happened  
- Screenshots or logs if available  

Your feedback helps improve the platform for churches around the world.
